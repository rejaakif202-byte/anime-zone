Telegram Links Guide
Season-wise:
Agar ek season ke saare episodes ek Telegram channel/post mein hain — Season Telegram Links use karo. Ye link sab episodes pe dikhega.

Episode-wise:
Agar har episode ka alag Telegram link hai — Episode Telegram Links use karo. Ye season link ko override kar deta hai.

Quality-wise:
Alag alag quality ke alag Telegram messages/files hain to har quality field fill karo.
Agar ek hi link sab ke liye hai to "Single Link" field use karo.
Telegram Link kaise milega?
1. Telegram pe message/file pe long press karo
2. "Copy link" ya "Share" option select karo
3. Woh link yahan paste karo

Note: Channel/group public hona chahiye ya user ko join karna hoga.
